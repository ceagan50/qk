How to Get Here

We're conveniently located opposite KCB Bank in Ndeeba along the Kampala-Masaka road. Here are some transportation options:

By Private Vehicle

Ample parking space available in front of our branch. Look for the QK SACCO signage.

By Taxi

Take a taxi to Ndeeba and ask to be dropped at KCB Bank. We're directly opposite.

By Public Transport

Board any bus or matatu going to Masaka and alight at Ndeeba stage. A 2-minute walk to our location.

By Boda Boda

Tell the rider "Quadral Kay SACCO opposite KCB Ndeeba." Fare is approximately 5,000 UGX from city center.
